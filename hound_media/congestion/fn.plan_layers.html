pub fn plan_layers(requests: &[QualityRequest], source_bps: u64) -> LayerPlan
Expand description

Aggregate every receiver’s QualityRequest into the sender’s LayerPlan.

The whole demand-driven, coalesced, source-droppable policy in one pure function:

  • a receiver wanting Source (or a cap ≥ source — it can only ask for lower) keeps the source alive;
  • receivers wanting less than source collapse to ONE shared low layer at the minimum of their caps, so the weakest can still decode it (bounded to two total streams — no per-receiver explosion);
  • if NO receiver can take the source, send_source is false and only the low layer is published — the optimisation that stops encoding a stream nobody wants.

With no receivers the source stays on (idle default; nothing to optimise yet). Pure/sans-IO → unit-tested below.
