§Congestion control + adaptive quality

Sans-IO pieces for graceful degradation under congestion:

  • RateController — a loss-based AIMD bandwidth estimator (the well-known WebRTC loss controller): increase slowly when loss is low, hold in the middle band, and back off fast when loss is high. Produces a target send bitrate.
  • QualityLadder — maps an available bitrate to a discrete video Rung with hysteresis (drop fast, climb slow) and a manual ceiling, falling back to Quality::AudioOnly when even the lowest rung won’t fit.

Principle: audio is sacred, video is the release valve. This governs video only; audio runs at a fixed low bitrate underneath and is never starved. Both types are pure and clock-driven for deterministic tests.
