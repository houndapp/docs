pub fn screen_rungs(quality: ScreenQuality) -> Vec<Rung>
Expand description

Build the screen-share rungs for a ScreenQuality, sans-IO and pure.

Candidate heights/fps are filtered to the caps, then assembled into one ascending-bitrate ladder whose order of climb encodes the degrade priority:

  • DegradePriority::Legibility climbs resolution at the floor fps, then fps at the cap resolution — so descending drops fps before resolution. Floor resolution is 720p (below that text is unreadable) unless the cap is lower.
  • DegradePriority::Motion climbs fps at the floor resolution, then resolution at the cap fps — so descending drops resolution before fps. Floor fps is 30 (below that it isn’t “motion”) unless the cap is lower; floor resolution is 480p.

The result is sorted ascending by bitrate (the ladder invariant) and deduplicated, so callers get a clean monotonic ladder for any cap combination.
