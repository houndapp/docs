§hound-media

Shared, sans-IO media layer for Hound voice/video: the on-the-wire packet format and the pure frame packetization / reassembly logic, with no network, codec, async, or clock dependencies.

§Why

The client and the SFU both speak this wire format; independent copies of the framing logic inevitably drift and disagree. This crate is the single source of truth for the wire contract, so the pieces cannot disagree, and every byte is covered by golden + round-trip tests that fail loudly if the format changes.

§Layers

  • packet — the fixed-size packet::PacketHeader and its enums (packet::TrackKind, packet::Codec), with byte-exact encode/decode.
  • fragment — fragment::Packetizer (split an encoded frame into MTU-sized packets) and fragment::Reassembler (collect a frame’s fragments back, tolerating reorder, duplicates, and loss).
  • clock — clock::CaptureClock, one monotonic timestamp source shared by audio and video capture so their timestamps share a timeline (A/V sync).
  • jitter — jitter::PlayoutBuffer, releases frames in timestamp order on a playout schedule (absorbs jitter/reorder; not gated on audio).
  • recovery — recovery::NackGenerator / recovery::RetransmitBuffer, NACK-based loss recovery (repair lost packets without waiting for a keyframe).
  • keyframe — keyframe::KeyframeRequester / keyframe::KeyframeThrottle, PLI keyframe requests + coalesced, rate-limited keyframe generation (the recovery path when loss is unrepairable by NACK).
  • congestion — congestion::RateController (loss-based bandwidth estimate) + congestion::QualityLadder (adaptive video rung selection).
  • send_budget — send_budget::SendBudget (AIMD estimate of the shared connection’s total send rate from aggregate publish-drop backpressure) + send_budget::allocate_send_budget (max-min-fair split across the local video tracks with a floor), so the per-track controllers can’t oversubscribe one shared QUIC uplink.
  • sim — a deterministic network-impairment simulator (loss/jitter/ reorder/duplication/bandwidth) for testing the engine without sockets.

Transport (iroh), congestion control, jitter buffering, and codecs live elsewhere and are pumped with the types defined here.
