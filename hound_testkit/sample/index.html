Test-facing glue over [hound_client::call_health]’s steady-state audio-starvation monitor. houndbot’s CI verdict and crate::call::InCall::expect_steady_audio both gate on the same hound-client type, so a call that passes one passes the other by construction — this module just adapts it to a &HoundClient.

Structs§

Sample
One observation window’s rollup. Peer/frame counts are high-water marks over the whole window (a peer that briefly joined/left, or a frame count that can only grow, are both captured correctly by tracking a running max rather than reading only the final sample); starved is the warmup-gated verdict.
