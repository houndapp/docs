1//! # hound-media
2//!
3//! Shared, **sans-IO** media layer for Hound voice/video: the on-the-wire packet
4//! format and the pure frame packetization / reassembly logic, with no network,
5//! codec, async, or clock dependencies.
6//!
7//! ## Why
8//!
9//! The client and the SFU both speak this wire format; independent copies of
10//! the framing logic inevitably drift and disagree. This crate is the single
11//! source of truth for the wire contract, so the pieces cannot disagree, and
12//! every byte is covered by golden + round-trip tests that fail loudly if the
13//! format changes.
14//!
15//! ## Layers
16//!
17//! * [`packet`] — the fixed-size [`packet::PacketHeader`] and its enums
18//!   ([`packet::TrackKind`], [`packet::Codec`]), with byte-exact encode/decode.
19//! * [`fragment`] — [`fragment::Packetizer`] (split an encoded frame into
20//!   MTU-sized packets) and [`fragment::Reassembler`] (collect a frame's
21//!   fragments back, tolerating reorder, duplicates, and loss).
22//! * [`clock`] — [`clock::CaptureClock`], one monotonic timestamp source shared
23//!   by audio and video capture so their timestamps share a timeline (A/V sync).
24//! * [`jitter`] — [`jitter::PlayoutBuffer`], releases frames in timestamp order
25//!   on a playout schedule (absorbs jitter/reorder; not gated on audio).
26//! * [`recovery`] — [`recovery::NackGenerator`] / [`recovery::RetransmitBuffer`],
27//!   NACK-based loss recovery (repair lost packets without waiting for a keyframe).
28//! * [`keyframe`] — [`keyframe::KeyframeRequester`] / [`keyframe::KeyframeThrottle`],
29//!   PLI keyframe requests + coalesced, rate-limited keyframe generation (the
30//!   recovery path when loss is unrepairable by NACK).
31//! * [`congestion`] — [`congestion::RateController`] (loss-based bandwidth
32//!   estimate) + [`congestion::QualityLadder`] (adaptive video rung selection).
33//! * [`send_budget`] — [`send_budget::SendBudget`] (AIMD estimate of the shared
34//!   connection's total send rate from aggregate publish-drop backpressure) +
35//!   [`send_budget::allocate_send_budget`] (max-min-fair split across the local
36//!   video tracks with a floor), so the per-track controllers can't oversubscribe
37//!   one shared QUIC uplink.
38//! * [`sim`] — a deterministic network-impairment simulator (loss/jitter/
39//!   reorder/duplication/bandwidth) for testing the engine without sockets.
40//!
41//! Transport (iroh), congestion control, jitter buffering, and codecs live
42//! elsewhere and are pumped with the types defined here.
