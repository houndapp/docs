1//! # hound-sfu
2//!
3//! The SFU (Selective Forwarding Unit) library: [`moq_relay`] (the forward-only
4//! MoQ plane and the per-connection auth gate), [`sfu`] (the endpoint-bind +
5//! accept-loop glue production and tests both drive), and [`auth`] (relay-token
6//! verification, re-exported from [`hound_types::tokens::relay`] so the
7//! backend's mint and this crate's verify are the exact same formula).
8//!
9//! `main.rs` is the binary shell around this library: CLI/config, secret-key
10//! loading, the health HTTP endpoint, and the backend self-registration client.
11//! Nothing in `lib.rs` and its modules touches a CLI flag, an env var, or the
12//! filesystem.
13
14pub mod auth;
15pub mod moq_relay;
16pub mod sfu;
