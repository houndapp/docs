Expand description

A running per-application audio source delivering fixed-size 48 kHz mono PCM frames — the audio leg of a ScreenCapture window/app share.

Deliberately the same contract as AudioInput (the mic path): 20 ms crate::types::SAMPLES_PER_FRAME-sample 48 kHz mono frames stamped on the capture clock, so the Opus encoder and publish path reuse the mic plumbing without a special case. Whatever the platform delivers (SCK hands back deinterleaved float32 stereo at the configured rate), the backend downmixes to mono and resamples to 48 kHz off the capture callback before delivery.

§Implementor contract

  • Self::next_frame blocks until the next frame, or returns Err when the underlying screen capture ends (the audio leg dies with its video parent).
  • !Send, same threading contract as the parent ScreenCapture.
