Expand description

A video decoder: encoded chunks in, raw frames out. The receive-side complement to VideoEncode; one per inbound video track.

§Implementor contract

  • Self::decode takes one encoded access unit and returns zero or one decoded RawFrame. Zero is normal and not an error: a decoder cannot emit anything until it has seen the in-band bootstrap (H.264 SPS/PPS, AV1 sequence header), so pre-keyframe chunks are dropped.
  • Decoded frames are PixelFormat::I420 (backends that decode to NV12 natively convert before returning) at the decoded picture dimensions.

fn backend(&self) -> &'static str

A short human label for the decode path actually in use, including whether it is hardware- or software-backed (e.g. "VideoToolbox (hardware)", "openh264 (software)"). For diagnostics — surfaced in the call logs so a CPU-burning software-decode fallback (e.g. AV1 with no HW block) is visible.
