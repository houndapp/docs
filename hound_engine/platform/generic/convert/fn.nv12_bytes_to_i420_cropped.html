NV12 → tightly-packed I420 with a display crop.

The source planes are laid out at coded_height rows (the codec’s macroblock-aligned height) with y_stride bytes/row; only the top-left out_width × out_height is real picture — H.264/HEVC pad the alignment to the bottom/right and carry the true size as the display aperture (MF_MT_MINIMUM_DISPLAY_APERTURE). The interleaved CbCr plane begins at y_stride * coded_height (NOT * out_height), so the coded height is what places the chroma; the output is cropped to out_*. out_width/out_height must be even and within the coded frame. Returns None on malformed input.
