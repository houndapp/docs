§Synthetic audio source (mic-free test source)

An [AudioInput] that fabricates a continuous sine-wave tone at the engine’s fixed 20 ms/48 kHz mono PCM contract, without any microphone — the audio counterpart to [super::synthetic::SyntheticCapturer]. Lets a headless client (a bot with no audio hardware) still publish a mic track.

§Timing contract

Mirrors [super::synthetic::SyntheticCapturer::next_frame]: [Self::next_frame] paces itself to one [SAMPLES_PER_FRAME]-sample frame by sleeping the remainder of the 20 ms interval since the last emit — so the delivered cadence reflects real time (a real mic’s callback rate) rather than free-running as fast as the caller pulls.
