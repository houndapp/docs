pub(crate) fn downmix_f32_planar_to_mono_i16(

Expand description

Downmix one block of deinterleaved (planar) float32 audio — channels separate same-length slices — to a single mono i16 stream, appending to out.

This is the exact shape ScreenCaptureKit delivers app audio in: an AudioBufferList of N non-interleaved float32 buffers. We average the channels per sample and convert the -1.0..=1.0 float to i16 with saturation, the format the crate::backend::AppAudio / mic contract carries. A single channel is just a per-sample convert (the average of one).

Empty planes (or all-empty planes) appends nothing. Planes of unequal length are tolerated: the per-sample average uses only the planes that still have a sample at that index (so a short final plane never reads out of bounds), and the frame length is the longest plane.
