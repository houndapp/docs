pub const SILENCE_FLOOR: i32 = 328;

Peak |sample| (i16 scale, 0..=32767) below which released audio is treated as silence / comfort noise rather than speech. ~328 ≈ 1% of full scale ≈ −40 dBFS: well above comfort-noise / dither, well below any intelligible speech peak.
