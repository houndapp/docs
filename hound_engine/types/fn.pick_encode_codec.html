pub fn pick_encode_codec(
    local_encode: VideoCodecCaps,
    common_decode: VideoCodecCaps,
) -> VideoCodec
Expand description

Choose which codec to encode: the highest-priority codec this device can encode (local_encode) AND every receiver can decode (common_decode).

Falls back to H.264 when no higher rung is common — H.264 is always encodable (HW or SW openh264) and decodable, so a call never loses video for lack of a shared codec. common_decode is the intersection of every current video receiver’s advertised decode caps (see VideoCodecCaps::intersect); it shrinks as less-capable peers join and grows back as they leave, which is what drives re-negotiation.
