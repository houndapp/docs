Expand description

Open an app-scoped share-audio leg for identity, delivering the standard 48 kHz mono 20 ms frames (ScreenAudio::next_frame). Pair it with a window share whose audio the portal could not scope automatically.

Err on an empty identity or an unreachable PipeWire daemon; an identity that currently matches no streams opens successfully and starts delivering when the app plays. !Send — drive and drop it on the share’s capture thread, like every audio leg.
