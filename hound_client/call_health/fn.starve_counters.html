Every starvation signal sample_call_health gates on: device-side output underruns/mix-starves plus each peer’s jitter-buffer underflow/late-drop/ overflow-drop/loss counters. Cross-references crate::audio_report::diagnose’s per-stage rules — this sums the same counters into one pass/fail total rather than attributing a cause. (device-output underruns, mix/jitter starve events) — judged separately: output underruns get no budget, mix/jitter ticks get STEADY_STARVE_BUDGET.
