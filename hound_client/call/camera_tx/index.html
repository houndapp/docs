Module camera_tx

Expand description

Outbound camera: the !Send capture+encode thread (device hot-swap, self-preview tee, adaptive encode) and the async publish task running the 1 s BWE adaptation tick. The capture side reads its decisions off adapt::AdaptShared; the codec is negotiated against the roster and re-driven mid-call by the re-negotiator. Self-preview keeps running even while the ladder pauses video (AudioOnly), so the local tile never freezes.

cam_capture_encode_loop 🔒
The camera capture+encode loop. Mirrors crates/frb/src/camera.rs for the self-preview push, and additionally H.264-encodes for publish. Honors the enabled flag (released device when off) and hot-swaps on device_rx change. Reads adapt each frame: retargets the encoder bitrate, downsamples to the rung fps, downscales to the rung geometry, serves a forced IDR, and skips encode/publish while paused (AudioOnly under severe congestion). Self-preview keeps running even while paused.
open_cam 🔒
Open the camera per capture_source: real hardware at CAM_DIMS@CAM_FPS, or (behind synthetic) the engine’s camera-free test pattern. The one seam cam_capture_encode_loop’s (re)open routes through.
spawn_cam_publish 🔒
Spawn the camera publish task. When enabled, a blocking thread opens the engine Camera, pushes each frame’s I420 to the self-preview sink ("self"), H.264-encodes it, and queues the access units for the async publish half. When disabled, capture stops (the device is released). Self-preview works even if the publish/subscribe never gets a remote peer.
