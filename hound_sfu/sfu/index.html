§SFU service endpoint

This module owns the iroh Endpoint and the accept() loop. It is thin glue: every accepted connection is handed to moq_relay::handle_relay_connection, which authenticates the relay token, admits the session against moq_relay::SessionLimits, scopes the connection to its space, and drives the moq-net session. All forwarding logic lives in crate::moq_relay; this module just binds the endpoint and dispatches connections to it.
