Sustained-rate ingress budget: a pure, synchronous policy answering “has this connection’s ingress rate stayed over the cap across the whole sliding window?” from periodic cumulative-byte samples.

A single high sample (a keyframe burst, a momentary retransmit storm) must not trip the budget — only a rate sustained across the entire configured window does. crate::moq_relay’s per-connection watchdog drives this by feeding it conn.stats().udp_rx.bytes (cumulative QUIC ingress bytes) on a timer; this type itself stays free of any iroh/tokio dependency so the policy is unit-testable with synthetic timestamps.
