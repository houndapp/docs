Handle one incoming MoQ relay connection on a shared origin.

This is the per-connection relay logic factored out of Relay::run so the SFU service’s accept loop (crate::sfu::Sfu::run) can drive the relay with the SFU’s shared origin, relay secret, and session registry. Relay::run (used by the standalone relay tests) and the SFU loop both call it, so they exercise identical auth/admission/scoping/session glue.
