§hound-sfu

The SFU (Selective Forwarding Unit) library: moq_relay (the forward-only MoQ plane and the per-connection auth gate), sfu (the endpoint-bind + accept-loop glue production and tests both drive), and auth (relay-token verification, re-exported from [hound_types::tokens::relay] so the backend’s mint and this crate’s verify are the exact same formula).

main.rs is the binary shell around this library: CLI/config, secret-key loading, the health HTTP endpoint, and the backend self-registration client. Nothing in lib.rs and its modules touches a CLI flag, an env var, or the filesystem.
