Expand description

A pending email magic-link login: the bound loopback listener + the port/state the caller hands the backend as the email link’s callback. Unlike BrowserLogin, this never opens (or even builds) a browser URL — the link lives in the email, minted server-side, and carries the port/state straight into it. Consume with wait (or drop to cancel).
